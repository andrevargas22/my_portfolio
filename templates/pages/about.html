About — André Vargas, machine learning & MLOps engineer in Brasília.

Mechatronics. Hardware. Data. Then machine learning — most of it self-taught.

I'm a machine learning engineer at Grupo Casas Bahia, one of Brazil's largest retailers. My path started in mechatronics engineering at Universidade de Brasília — with an exchange year at Cardiff University — and a first job building hardware: IoT, industrial automation, and embedded firmware.

A fascination with data pulled me toward data science, and then toward MLOps — building and deploying ML systems, designing cloud architectures, writing CI/CD pipelines, and shipping production models and APIs.

I'm largely self-taught, and I like it that way. I built this site from scratch — HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, all learned on my own. With the resources we have today, I think you can learn just about anything you decide to.

Outside work: my wife Maria, our dogs Bibi and Quico, the drums, and a slightly excessive catalogue of finished games.
