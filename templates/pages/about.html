About me

My journey began with a degree in Mechatronics Engineering from the Universidade de Brasília, which provided a strong foundation in electronics, microcontrollers, hardware design, and embedded firmware programming. An exchange program at Cardiff University further broadened my technical and cultural horizons.

Initially, I worked as a Hardware Engineer, focusing on IoT, industrial automation and digital transformation. After a few years, my fascination with data and its potential to drive innovation led me to pivot towards Data Science and Machine Learning.

Currently I work as a Machine Learning Engineer, with experience in developing and deploying ML and MLOps solutions with a solid background in Computer Science and Software Engineering, cloud architectures, CI/CD, and implementing models and APIs in production.

I am passionate about studying and am largely self-taught. Despite not being a web designer by trade, I designed and built this website myself, incorporating cool features using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, all of which I learned on my own.

Outside of work, I share my life with my wife, Maria, whose support inspires me daily, and our dog, Bibi. I'm an avid gamer and music enthusiast, often found behind a drum set. These passions enrich my life, providing balance and perspective that I bring into my work.
